Our Services

Explore the wide range of services we provide to ensure quality cancer care at every stage.

Genetic Testing

Identify inherited cancer risks for proactive prevention and early detection.

Genetic testing helps identify individuals with inherited gene mutations that increase cancer risk. This allows for early surveillance and preventive care.

Rehabilitation Therapy

Recover strength and improve quality of life with personalized physical therapies.

Our rehab programs include physiotherapy and occupational therapy to restore mobility, reduce fatigue, and enhance independence.

Clinical Trials

Access to cutting-edge research treatments through safe clinical studies.

Be part of medical breakthroughs with our monitored clinical trial participation. It offers new treatment options before they are widely available.

Pain Management

Advanced pain relief techniques for enhanced patient comfort and dignity.

We provide tailored pain relief plans including medications, nerve blocks, and palliative interventions to ensure patient comfort.

Holistic Therapies

Complementary healing practices including yoga, acupuncture, and meditation.

We integrate mind-body approaches to support emotional wellness and reduce stress during cancer treatment and recovery.

Diagnosis & Screening

Early detection saves lives. Our advanced technology helps diagnose accurately.

We offer state-of-the-art imaging, pathology, and screening tests for timely and precise cancer detection.

Radiation Therapy

Targeted treatments that destroy cancer cells while preserving healthy tissue.

Advanced machines like linear accelerators deliver focused radiation, reducing side effects and improving outcomes.

Chemotherapy

Effective medication to eliminate or control cancer spread.

Customized drug combinations are provided with close monitoring to treat cancer and reduce recurrence.

Post-Treatment Care

Support, monitoring, and recovery care for long-term health and well-being.

We guide patients with follow-up schedules, recovery nutrition, lifestyle changes, and long-term health planning.

Palliative Care

Compassionate care to improve the quality of life for patients with serious illnesses.

Our palliative team manages symptoms, provides emotional support, and assists with complex care decisions.

Immunotherapy

Boosting the body's immune system to fight cancer naturally and effectively.

We offer cutting-edge immunotherapy drugs like checkpoint inhibitors and CAR-T cells based on tumor profiling.

Nutritional Support

Customized nutrition plans to support strength, immunity, and healing.

Our dietitians provide meal planning and supplementation to maintain weight, energy, and overall immunity.

Counseling & Mental Support

Emotional care and mental health support for patients and their families.

Psychological therapy, support groups, and grief counseling are offered to help you and your family cope.
